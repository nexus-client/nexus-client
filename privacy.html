Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Nexus Client handles information related to the use of the software and website. Nexus Client aims to minimize data collection wherever possible.

2. Data Collection

Nexus Client currently does not automatically collect:

  • analytics data
  • telemetry
  • diagnostic reports
  • hardware information
  • usage tracking information
  • automatic crash reports

No personal information is sold to third parties.

Nexus Client does not currently use cookies, analytics, telemetry, advertising trackers, or automatic crash reporting.

3. Microsoft Authentication

Nexus Client may use Microsoft authentication services to allow users to access Minecraft-related functionality.

This may involve:

  • authentication tokens
  • Minecraft profile information
  • usernames
  • UUIDs

Authentication data is generally handled locally on the user's device where possible. Microsoft services are governed by Microsoft's own privacy policies and terms.

Nexus Client does not send Minecraft profile information, usernames, UUIDs or authentication-related data to its own servers. Authentication-related information may be processed locally as required for sign-in and Minecraft ownership verification.

4. Local Data Storage

Nexus Client may locally store:

  • launcher settings
  • configuration files
  • authentication or session information
  • cosmetic preferences
  • installed mod or launcher data

This information is stored on the user's own device for functionality purposes.

5. Website Data

The Nexus Client website currently:

  • does not use analytics
  • does not use advertising trackers
  • does not intentionally use tracking cookies
  • does not operate user accounts

The website is hosted using GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical connection data according to its own policies.

6. Third-Party Services

Nexus Client may rely on third-party services including:

  • Microsoft
  • Mojang/Minecraft services
  • GitHub

Use of those services may involve additional data processing governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep Nexus Client secure. However, no software or online service can guarantee absolute security.

Users are responsible for protecting:

  • their devices
  • their accounts
  • their credentials

8. Children's Privacy

Users must comply with Microsoft, Mojang and local legal age requirements. Nexus Client is not specifically directed toward children under applicable minimum age requirements.

9. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated over time. Changes become effective once published.
