1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information Nexus Client may collect, how that information is used, and what choices users have.

2. Information We May Collect

Nexus Client may use Microsoft authentication so users can sign in with their Microsoft account for Minecraft-related authentication. Depending on the authentication flow, the application may receive basic account information required for login, such as account identifiers or authentication tokens.

Nexus Client does not ask for your Microsoft password. Authentication is handled through Microsoft’s official sign-in system.

3. Minecraft Account Data

Nexus Client may access Minecraft-related account information only as needed to verify access and allow the client to function. Nexus Client does not sell Minecraft account data.

4. Local Data

Nexus Client may store settings locally on your device, such as client preferences, configuration options, or cached session information.

5. How We Use Information

Information is used only to provide and improve Nexus Client, including authentication, client functionality, debugging, security, and user support.

6. Data Sharing

Nexus Client does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when required to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, prevent abuse, or protect users and the project.

7. Security

We aim to handle user data carefully and limit collection to what is necessary. However, no software or online service can guarantee perfect security.

8. Children

Nexus Client is intended to be used only in accordance with Minecraft, Microsoft, and local legal requirements. Younger users should use the client only with permission from a parent or guardian where required.
