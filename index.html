About

Nexus Client is an independent Minecraft client and launcher designed to provide a cleaner and more optimized Minecraft experience while remaining compatible with multiple modding ecosystems.

Planned support includes Vanilla, Fabric, Forge and NeoForge.

Nexus Client does not provide Minecraft itself. Users must own a valid copy of Minecraft and use a legitimate Microsoft/Minecraft account.

Privacy

Nexus Client currently does not collect analytics, telemetry, diagnostic reports, hardware information or automatic crash reports.

Microsoft authentication may be used for Minecraft-related functionality. Some authentication and configuration data may be stored locally on the user's device.
